About

Ritewood properties Realty, Lcc was founded by Solly Idehen, due to his long passion for contrustion. Solly Idehen had an Electrical Engineering degree from the university. He worked for Schlumberger as an international staff for some years. With his work experiences internationally and locally with Executive Homes Realty, LLC, he decided to pursue his passion for construction by establishing Ritewood Properties Realty, LLC.

Ritewood properties was established with the main purpose of building "Dream homes" for home buyers in Atlanta, Georgia.
